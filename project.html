Description: This is an ongoing project. As being selected as a sponsored team, we receive $100,000 stipend to start an AI socialBot research. Our ambition is to eliminate the frustrating experience in the interactions with existing sicalBots. Our socialbot is meant to support daily conversation with human by using a sophisticated context tracking model. Its basic functionality is generating and recording important idea, concepts or topics during a conversation, to maintain the coherence of chatting. More importantly, our socialbot should be able to detect context distraction, context missing and context combination from the speaker and use it as guidance for generating responses. Role: Software Engineer, Engineering Team Size: about 20

Description: Zuoye Bang is an educational mobile application that assists elementary and secondary school students in seeking instructions, studying related topics and finding similar exercises when they encounter difficult problems. The mission of the product is to eliminate the imbalance of educational resources among different areas. The core technologies of this project include optical character recognition (OCR), information retrieval, data mining, multimedia processing. I was responsible for the backend search system in this project. As of August 2015, the product had attracted 50 million users, accumulated 95 million exercises, and responded to 4 billion questions. The product was adopted by 113 educational institutions and 370,000 elementary and secondary schools. Baidu had demerged this product into a new company assessed at one hundred million dollars. Zuoye Bang is now the leading mobile educational product in the Chinese market.

Role: Software Engineer, Engineering Team Size: about 15

Description: As we know, China has the largest population in the world, and there are only limited healthcare resources. It might spend patients several hours or even a day waiting in the queue but only get a diagnosis from doctors within several minutes. When users are not feeling well about their health, they might want to ask for suggestions before going to the hospital. If it is not a serious problem, they can take care of themselves. If they need further examinations, they also want suggestions on which department of the hospital they should go. Within our platform, users can log in the website and describe their symptoms online. I was responsible for the core data flow in this project, including data processing, data storage, and data analysis.

Role: Software Engineer, Engineering Team Size: about 50

Description: Baidu Zhidao is an online knowledge sharing community. At each question page, this is a recommendation section, which recommends a list of related questions and answers. I refactored the recommendation system by adding a recommendation engine based on users' behavior, which improved the click rate by 7% in the AB test. The major challenges of this project included big data analysis (abstracting fectures from one billion question-answer pairs), data storage (implementing index structure to store one billion nodes in a graph to provide efficient recommendation service.), system design (refactoring the recommendation system to integrate the new engine without affecting the original service.).

Go to: Baidu Zhidao
